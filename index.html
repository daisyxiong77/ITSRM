Intrinsic Temporally Smoothed Semiparametric Regression Model

Supplementary Appendix

This website provides the supplementary appendix for the proposed Intrinsic Temporally Smoothed Semiparametric Regression Model (ITSRM).

The supplementary appendix contains the detailed derivation of the proposed ITSRM and the corresponding iterative optimization algorithm for jointly estimating the parametric components (pt, Vt) and the nonparametric component gt(·).

Contents

  • Detailed derivation of the proposed ITSRM.
  • Estimation of the parametric components.
  • Estimation of the nonparametric component.
  • Iterative optimization algorithm for model estimation.
