Supplementary Appendix

This website provides the supplementary appendix for the paper Intrinsic Semiparametric Analysis of Dynamic Brain Functional Connectivity on the SPD Manifold.

The proposed method is referred to as the Intrinsic Temporally Smoothed Semiparametric Regression Model (ITSRM). The supplementary appendix includes two main parts. Appendix A introduces the manifold geometry of SPD matrices. Appendix B presents the intrinsic derivation of the proposed dimensionality reduction method under the affine-invariant Riemannian metric.

Contents

  • Appendix A. Manifold Geometry of SPD Matrices.
  • Appendix B. Intrinsic Derivation of the Proposed Dimensionality Reduction under the Affine-Invariant Riemannian Metric.
