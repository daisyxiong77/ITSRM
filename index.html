Supplementary Appendix

This website provides the supplementary appendix for the proposed Intrinsic Temporally Smoothed Semiparametric Regression Model (ITSRM).

The supplementary appendix includes two main parts. Appendix A presents the intrinsic derivation of the proposed dimensionality reduction method under the affine-invariant Riemannian metric. Appendix B provides the iterative estimation algorithm for ITSRM.

Contents

  • Appendix A. Intrinsic Derivation of the Proposed Dimensionality Reduction under the Affine-Invariant Riemannian Metric.
  • Appendix B. Iterative Estimation Algorithm for ITSRM.
